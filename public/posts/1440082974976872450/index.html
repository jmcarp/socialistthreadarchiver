boyles says he has spoken with 6 police officers and "other people as well," "regional law enforcement leaders." he made his decision to fire the police chief after speaking with only 6 officers within the department. he won't disclose who or what they talked about.
the "other people" he spoke to were likely employees at the ECC, UVA, and albemarle county. it's worth noting that the UVA police chief used to be the cville police chief & he did some not great things here! https://t.co/fIhfV4DZlG

"without the respect and without the following and the the confidence of the officers of the police department that the rest of the direction, which had been going in a positive direction, could not be completed," boyles stammers out.

myra anderson says she is concerned about the mental health effects on residents in the neighborhoods where these gun violent incidents have been taking place. as part of these conversations about funding, she wants council to remember mental health & trauma.
